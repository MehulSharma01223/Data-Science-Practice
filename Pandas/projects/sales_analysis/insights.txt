SALES ANALYSIS PROJECT - INSIGHTS

1. City-wise Sales:
   - Delhi shows strong total sales performance.
   - Mumbai also contributes significantly to overall revenue.
   - City-wise sales help identify high-performing markets.

2. Product Performance:
   - Laptop generates high revenue because of its higher selling price.
   - Phones show strong sales activity across multiple cities.
   - Product-wise sales help understand which products drive revenue.

3. Category-wise Performance:
   - Computers and Mobiles contribute strongly to total sales.
   - Accessories generate lower revenue compared to other categories.
   - Category analysis helps decide business focus areas.

4. Quantity Analysis:
   - Some products sell more units but may generate lower revenue.
   - High quantity does not always mean high sales value.
   - Quantity analysis helps compare demand and revenue together.

5. Payment Mode Analysis:
   - Payment mode analysis helps understand customer payment preferences.
   - UPI, Card, and Cash transactions show different sales contributions.
   - This can help businesses improve payment strategy.

6. Product Contribution:
   - Pie chart analysis shows each product/category contribution to total sales.
   - Higher-value products contribute more to revenue share.

7. Quantity vs Sales:
   - Scatter plot helps compare quantity sold with sales amount.
   - Sales depend not only on quantity but also on product price.

8. City vs Product Sales:
   - Different cities show different product sales patterns.
   - This helps understand location-based product demand.

9. Final Conclusion:
   - This project covers groupby analysis, aggregation, pivot tables, sorting, visualization, and business insight writing.
   - It forms a complete beginner-to-intermediate level sales analytics workflow using Pandas and Matplotlib.
